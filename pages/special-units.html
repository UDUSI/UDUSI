TEMC Celsius temperature
Named SI unit
Definition: The unit of Celsius temperature is the degree Celsius, °C, which is equal in magnitude to the kelvin, K, the unit of thermodynamic temperature. The quantity Celsius temperature $t$ is related to thermodynamic temperature $T$ by the equation $t/\text{°C} = T/\text{K} − 273.15$.
Note: Unit definition is rewritten to connect "Celsius temperature" to thermodynamic temperature according to UDUNITS2 grammar.
